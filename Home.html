Welcome to Sylhet!

Sylhet, The Grand Tea View Resort ...Sylhet is home to several resorts, with luxury options like the five-star Grand Sylhet Hotel & Resort and the Grand Sultan Tea Resort & Golf in Srimangal, a region known for its tea gardens. These resorts offer a variety of amenities, including swimming pools, spas, fitness centers, and dining, while the Srimangal resort also features a golf course and movie theater. Many resorts are praised for their comfort, services, and beautiful natural surroundings, making them popular choices for both leisure and business travelers.
